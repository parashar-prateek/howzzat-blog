Hi there! My name is Aayansh and I'm 9 years old.

Welcome to **Howzzat!** — my very own blog where I get to share all the things I love writing about.

## What you'll find here

I love reading about amazing people and their lives, so I'll be writing **biographies** of people who inspire me — from legendary cricketers to scientists, explorers, and more.

I also love making up stories — **adventure tales**, funny stories, and maybe even some mystery ones too!

## Why "Howzzat"?

If you know cricket, you know "Howzzat!" is what the bowler shouts when they think the batsman is out. I love cricket — especially bowling — so it felt like the perfect name for my blog.

Think of every post as me bowling a yorker your way. I hope some of them are yorkers that get you!

## Let's go!

I'm really excited to start writing and sharing my work here. Stay tuned for stories, biographies, and all sorts of fun stuff.

See you around!
