## Intro

Vivian Richards is one of the world's best cricket players. He plays for The West Indies. He is a batter. Vivian Richards is known for smashing 6's!

## Early Life

Sir Issac Vivian Alexander Richards was born on March 7 1952 in St. Johns, British Leeward Islands. His parents are Malcom and Gretel. Vivian's hometown is Antigua. His siblings are Donald and Mervyn.

Vivian got introduced to cricket at a very young age. The Richards practiced cricket with their father.

At just 18 years old Vivian Richards left school and joined Antigua cricket team.

## Middle Life

### Test debut against St Kitts

Vivian's debut for Antigua was against St Kitts and unfortunately for Vivian he got a golden duck. But Vivian didn't come on his bed or couch crying 'i got a golden duck i'm never playing cricket ever again in my whole life'. Instead he listened to his coach and went to the nets to fix what happened.

So Vivian chased his dreams of being a West Indian star batter but doesn't find shortcuts to them.

He felt a little embarrassed about the golden duck because he made about 6,000 fans sad.

The game was a test match so after an hour, In the second innings Vivian came to bat against St Kitts and got another duck!

He let 6,000 fans down again.

### Becoming a star in the West Indies premier league

Later on Richards played for 2 clubs in the West Indies premier League called St Johns. The second one is Antigua.

### West Indies!

In 1974 the big moment came!

Vivian Richards was selected for the West Indies!

His test debut was against India. It was a 5 match series against India.

In test 2 he scored an unbeaten 192.
